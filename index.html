Intro

I'm a 3rd year student pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I have a keen interest in web development and enjoy learning new technologies and frameworks.

I also have a keen interest in game development and have dabbled in the world of game engines such as Godot.

My hobbies include playing video games, watching movies and reading comics. One of my favorite game is Red Dead Redemption 2.

I've worked on some small projects over the years. While not something grandeous, I would like to think of those as a stepping stone for my journey as a computer engineer. You can check some of those here

Work

A quiz show which was presented by me for my 2nd year Java project. The "game" featured 3 levels and a scoring system. You can check it out here.

Besides coding, I also design as a side hobby. As a member of the UAP Indoor Games Club, I've worked on numerous posters, banners, social media posts. What you see left of me is a leaflet that was scattered among the students during club fair.

The work that I'm most proud of is the new logo of my club. It was renewed last year and has been 3D printed and hanged inside the indoor games room.
